About Us

Inozon Engineering (Pty) Ltd. is a recently launched mechanical engineering company specialising in precision machining and light fabrication. The company’s goal is to provide a quality service and precision machined products that make a real difference. Our director Innocent Masango, has a passion for working with steel and has 27 years’ experience working in mechanical engineering on conventional machines as well as CNC machines.
INOZON is currently involved in the design and manufacturing of Crushers and Hammer Mills for artisanal gold miners in Zimbabwe and new machinery dedicated to reprocessing gold tailings.
We have started putting together a team of highly skilled and experienced technicians to operate our conventional and rented CNC machines. The future looks bright and exciting and you can be assured of our attention to detail and commitment to quality.

Our range of machines include among others;

  • Conventional Lathe Machines
  • Conventional Milling Machines
  • Radial Arm Drilling Machines
  • Slotting Machines
  • Rented CNC Lathe Machine and a rented CNC Milling Machine
  • CO2 Welding

We pride ourselves in the quality of work that we produce
and please contact us if there are any machined components that you need.
